Mechanism A (tractable, scoped, dominant): `halfmax_edges` runs `merge_adjacent_runs` BEFORE the D-01/D-03 boundary-clip-exclusion filter. When a raw edge-touching run (correctly excludable on its own) sits within `MAX_RUN_MERGE_GAP_PIXELS=10`px of an otherwise-valid interior run, the merge fuses them and the WHOLE fused span is discarded as edge-touching -- silently swallowing a legitimate candidate. Measured: 65-90% of `no_candidates` invalidations across all 4 tracks trace to this mechanism; a merge-gap sweep (0/5/10/15/20/30px) shows swallow count increasing monotonically with gap size, confirming a structural ordering defect. Severity correlates with each track's own chronic y-strip-edge threshold-exceedance (track 10's y-index-0 leading edge: 94.8% of columns above threshold; track 21's y-index-479 trailing edge: 91.4%).

Mechanism B (tractable, scoped): `MIN_TRACKED_LENGTH_RATIO`'s same-column-relative gate (Amendment A7) provides no protection when a column has exactly one (small/spurious) candidate to compare against itself -- measured 14-72% of tracked selections across the 4 tracks occur in such single-candidate columns, producing frequent short (1-2 column) width blips (e.g. Track 8 x=24.1/24.5/24.7/25.5mm) that mechanically increase fragmentation even though each episode is shorter than pre-Amendment-A7.

Mechanism C (NOT tractable within this phase's scope, per the diagnosis's own HONEST-OUTCOME GUARD finding): greedy nearest-to-previous_center selection among multiple simultaneously-plausible candidates can still hop between distinct real features (measured up to 1.52mm raw hop at track 21's tail) -- this is the DP/Viterbi joint-tracker gap 01-14-PLAN.md already explicitly named and deferred as out of scope. No new tractable fix beyond what 01-14 already considered was found; this plan does NOT implement one, and does not re-open the already-accepted 10-vs-14 width-ordering FLAG (UAT Test 7).

Left-edge unassessable crop bands (all 4 tracks, ~x=20.0-22.3mm) are a separate, non-defective, already-locked native-data-availability limitation (MIN_COLUMNS_PER_BIN/MAX_GAP_PIXELS) -- not part of this defect class, no fix indicated.

This plan implements the diagnosis's two concretely-suggested, jointly-required fixes for Mechanisms A and B, verified directly against the exact diagnosed episodes, then regenerates all 4 tracks atomically and reports the fragmentation/jump-statistic/crop-edge outcome honestly -- explicitly anticipating Mechanism C's residual effect will NOT be eliminated.
